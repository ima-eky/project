About Us

Insights & Strategy Consulting Limited (ISCL) is a leading research, polling, and data analytics firm legally registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria.

Our unique approach combines innovative methodologies, advanced data analytics, and actionable insights to ensure that our clients make informed, impactful decisions

ISCL operates across various sectors, offering services that range from research and strategy development to monitoring and evaluation, data management, and policy analysis.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between data and decision-making, empowering organisations to optimise their operations, enhance customer experience, and achieve sustainable growth.
